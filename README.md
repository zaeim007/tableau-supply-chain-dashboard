# 📊 Global Supply Chain Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Tableau dashboard** designed to analyze global supply chain performance. It focuses on delivery efficiency, logistics delays, and their impact on business profitability.

The dashboard converts raw operational data into meaningful insights, helping identify inefficiencies and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Orders:** 180,519  
- **Late Delivery Rate:** 54.83%  
- **Average Shipping Delay:** 0.57 days  
- **Total Profit:** $3.96M  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- 📅 **Orders Trend Over Time** – Analyze demand patterns  
- 🚚 **Late Delivery by Shipping Mode** – Identify inefficient shipping methods  
- 🌍 **Late Delivery by Market** – Compare regional performance  
- 📊 **Shipping Delay Distribution** – Understand delay patterns  
- 💰 **Profit vs Shipping Delay** – Analyze business impact  
- 🗺 **Global Delivery Performance Map** – Visualize geographic insights  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Over **54% of deliveries are delayed**, indicating inefficiencies  
- Certain **shipping modes and regions** contribute more to delays  
- Most delays are short but frequent  
- Delivery delays impact **profitability**  
- Geographic analysis highlights **regional performance gaps**
